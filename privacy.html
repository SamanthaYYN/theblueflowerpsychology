Introduction
This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect any personal information you provide when using this website or accessing our voluntary services. Our platform provides online psychological support, educational resources, training, and advice. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect
We may collect the following personal information:
- Your name and email address (e.g., from contact forms, consent forms, or enquiries).
- Information shared during 1:1 psychological sessions, including any details necessary to provide personalised support.
- Feedback or evaluation information provided voluntarily by you.

How We Use Your Information
Your information is used solely to:
- Respond to your enquiries or requests for services.
- Provide and personalise 1:1 interventions, resources, or advice.
- Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of our services (data is anonymised wherever possible).

Legal Basis
We process personal data based on your consent. By contacting us or taking part in a session, you provide consent for us to use your information as described. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.

Sharing of Information
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Information may only be shared if required by law or to protect safety, such as in safeguarding concerns.

Data Storage & Security
We store your personal data securely, with access limited to those involved in providing the service. Data is kept only as long as necessary to deliver our services or meet legal obligations.
