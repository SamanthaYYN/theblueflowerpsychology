Why the Blue Flower?

It is a Forget-Me-Not flower, which is widely recognised as symbol of dementia awareness, representing memory, identity, and the importance of staying connected with a person even as their abilities change. At The Blue Flower Psychology,the Forget-Me-Not reflects our commitment to supporting people living with dementia, and those who care for them, with compassion, clarity, and understanding.


But for us, the meaning extends far beyond dementia...


The Forget-Me-Not is also a powerful reminder to not forget yourself. Many people - parents, professionals, caregivers, students, andanyone under pressure, can become overwhelmed, stressed, or emotionally exhausted. The flower symbolises the importance of remembering your own needs, caring for your emotional wellbeing, and recognising that your feelings matter, even when life is demanding.
